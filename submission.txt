PROJECT TITLE
-------------
Edge AI Plant Disease Detection System

DESCRIPTION
-----------
An offline, edge-deployable plant disease detection system that runs entirely
on a Raspberry Pi 5. A MobileNetV3-Small model is trained on the PlantVillage
dataset, exported to ONNX, and quantized to INT8 for real-time inference.
The system captures live video from the Pi Camera Module v2 and overlays the
predicted disease class and confidence score directly on the frame — no
internet connection required at inference time.
